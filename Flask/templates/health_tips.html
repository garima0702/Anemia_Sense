Health Tips for Anemia

What is Anemia?

Anemia is a condition where your body doesn't have enough healthy red blood cells to carry adequate oxygen to your tissues, making you feel tired and weak.

Common Symptoms

  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Pale or yellowish skin
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Headaches

Precautions

  • Get regular health checkups
  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in iron
  • Take iron supplements if prescribed
  • Avoid excessive tea/coffee (reduces iron absorption)
  • Stay hydrated

Foods to Help with Iron Deficiency

  • Spinach, kale, and other leafy greens
  • Red meat, poultry, and fish
  • Lentils, beans, and chickpeas
  • Fortified cereals
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Vitamin C-rich foods (to boost iron absorption)
